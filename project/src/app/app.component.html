Frequently Asked Questions

Custom men's clothing is an apparel category tailored for men. It includes t-shirts, hoodies, tank tops, sweatshirts, pants, shorts, long-sleeve shirts, sportswear, shoes, and more.

Partner with print-on-demand companies like Printify and get access to a large product scope. Sign up, pick your favorites, and start designing. Once sent to production – your print-on-demand men's clothing will be printed, packaged, and shipped by our Print Providers.

It depends on the selected printing method. Printify uses direct-to-garment (DTG), sublimation (AOP), direct-to-film (DTF), and embroidery printing for custom men's clothing. Once your products are designed and sent to production, the Print Provider takes over and prints, packages, and ships the items.
